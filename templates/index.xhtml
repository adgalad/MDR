Welcome to Mega Dash Raffle

We invite everyone to BUY tickets on these raffles to benefit projects for the Dash network.

For each ticket you buy, the value paid will be distributed (in % that may vary) to three wallets:

  • Jackpot for the raffle prize (40%)
  • Beneficiary project (50%)
  • Administration of the raffle system (10%)

Monthly, several raffle modalities will be created with different prices per ticket:

  • Weekly: Mini Dash Raffles (0.01 tDash) ideal for projects that seek funds to bring their proposals to Dash Boost
  • Biweekly: Dash Raffles (0.1 tDash): for projects seeking funds to bring their proposals to Dash Central
  • Monthly: Mega Dash Raffle (0.5 tDash): for projects authorized by Dash DAO that need funds to continue activities
